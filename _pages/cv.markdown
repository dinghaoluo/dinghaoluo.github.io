## research experience

<div class="cv-entry">
  <div class="cv-entry__head">
    <span class="cv-entry__org">Wang Lab, Max Planck Florida Institute for Neuroscience</span>
    <span class="cv-entry__loc">Jupiter, FL</span>
  </div>
  <div class="cv-entry__meta">
    <span class="cv-entry__role">Graduate researcher</span>
    <span class="cv-entry__date">May 2021 – Dec 2026 (expected)</span>
  </div>
  <p>I study how phasic locus coeruleus dopamine release tunes hippocampal CA1 dynamics during memory-guided navigation. The project combines virtual-reality behaviour, extracellular recordings, optogenetics, local pharmacology, two-photon dopamine and calcium imaging, Python analysis pipelines, and computational modelling.</p>
</div>
